This data was collected from Monkeypox data

Monkeypox is a disease that started in 1970 and has continued on into 2024. It is a rare disease seeing very few cases every year while being in 121 countries. When someone gets this disease they begin to get red lumps all over their body with a fever and nausea, it is a not very deadly disease with a death rate of about 0.04%.

The data set provided by the world health organization regarding mpox includeds dates by year month and days in 2022 and provides the information of how many new and total cases there have been and the deaths on that specific day in 2022.
